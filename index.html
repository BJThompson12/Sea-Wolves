How to Use

ArtiScapes is optimized for landscape orientation.

Your device is currently in portrait orientation.

  • To generate a new random landscape picture, click or tap the Generate Image button.
  • To use the timer option, type the minutes and seconds into the timer display and click or tap the Set Timer button. When the timer reaches 0, the image will automatically switch.
  • To hide most of the user interface (UI) and better see the full image, click the Hide UI button in the lower left corner. Click again to bring the UI back.
